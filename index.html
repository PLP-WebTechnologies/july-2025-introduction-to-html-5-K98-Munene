About Us

Our Mission

Secu Cam is a trusted leader in CCTV installation and security solutions, dedicated to protecting what matters most,your home, business, and peace of mind. Based in Kenya, we specialize in designing, installing, and maintaining high-quality surveillance systems tailored to meet the unique security needs of each client.
With a team of skilled technicians and a passion for innovation, we provide cutting-edge camera systems that deliver clear, reliable footage day or night. Whether it’s a small home setup or a complex commercial installation, Secu Cam ensures precision, professionalism, and unmatched customer service. We believe security should be smart, simple, and accessible. That’s why we partner with top brands and offer customized packages to suit every budget without compromising on quality.

Our Services

CCTV Installation (Home & Business)

We install high-definition CCTV cameras for homes, offices, shops, estates, and institutions. Our systems provide 24/7 monitoring with clear footage, both day and night.

Remote Viewing Setup

Access your CCTV system from anywhere using your phone, tablet, or computer. We configure secure remote access for real-time viewing and playback.

Site Survey & Security Consultation

Our team visits your premises to assess your security needs and recommend the best camera placement, system type, and coverage.

System Maintenance & Servicing

We offer routine checks, cleaning, system updates, and repairs to ensure your cameras remain effective and your recordings are secure.

Upgrades & System Expansion

Already have a CCTV system? We can upgrade old analog systems to modern IP-based solutions or add extra cameras for better coverage.
